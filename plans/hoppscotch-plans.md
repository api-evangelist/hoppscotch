# Hoppscotch Plans

Source: https://hoppscotch.com/pricing

## Free Plan

**Price:** $0/month (forever free)

Intended for individuals and small teams getting started with API testing.

### Includes
- Unlimited workspaces
- Unlimited collections
- Unlimited requests
- Unlimited runners
- Community support (Discord and GitHub)

---

## Organization Plan

**Price:** $6 per user/month (billed annually; monthly billing also available)

Crafted for teams and organizations seeking a full-featured API testing experience.

### Includes
- Everything in the Free plan
- Admin Dashboard for managing team members
- Dedicated support from the Hoppscotch team
- Custom payment options

---

## Enterprise Plan

**Price:** Custom (contact sales)

For larger organizations requiring advanced security, compliance, and dedicated capacity.

### Includes
- Everything in the Organization plan
- On-premises / self-hosted deployment
- SSO (Single Sign-On)
- Audit logs
- Contractual SLA
- Dedicated account management
- Custom integrations
